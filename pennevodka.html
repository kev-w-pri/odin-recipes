Penne Vodka

A delicious penne vodka is a great dish for families!

I remember the first time I had penne vodka. Actually, that is a blatant lie. My family has been eating penne vodka for as long as I can remember. I fucking love this shit. My mom's recipe is phenomenal. I can't find it right now, but still. It's amazing. Trust me.
